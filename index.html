Tensura

That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime, also known as Tensura, is a Japanese light novel series written by Fuse and illustrated by Mitz Vah. The series began as a web novel on the user-generated content site Shōsetsuka ni Narō in 2013 before being published in print by Micro Magazine with illustrations by Mitz Vah since 2014. As of October 2023, the series has been collected into 21 tankōbon volumes. The story follows Satoru Mikami, a 37-year-old corporate worker who is reincarnated in a fantasy world as a slime with unique abilities after being killed in a random stabbing incident.

Emnence in Shadow

Eminence in Shadow is a Japanese light novel series written by Daisuke Aizawa and illustrated by Touzai. The series began as a web novel on the user-generated content site Shōsetsuka ni Narō in 2018 before being published in print by Enterbrain with illustrations by Touzai since 2019. As of December 2023, the series has been collected into 7 tankōbon volumes. The story follows Cid Kagenou who aspires to become an "eminence in shadow," a person who operates behind the scenes to manipulate events and people without being noticed.
